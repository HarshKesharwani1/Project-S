Chapter 1

The Blooper Reel (My Bad)

Mistakes I committed after we became friends ki agar list banayein, toh bahut hi zyada lambi ho jayegi 📜. Kyunki kai baar I joke about something thinking ki wo funny hai, but wo actually funny nahi hota 🤦‍♂️. Kai baar main galat time pe joke kar deta hoon, aur agar tumhara mood thoda kharab hota hai, toh mere jokes se aur zyada kharab ho jata hai 😬. Aur fir mere mood swings... unka toh kya hi bolu, ladkiyon se zyada mood swings toh mereko ho jate hain 🎢. And last but not the least, my overthinking! 🧠 Uska toh kya hi bolu. Jitna tumko maine pareshaan kiya hai, utna shayad hi tumko kisi aur ne kiya hoga, aur na hi maine kisi aur ko kiya hai.

Par sach bolu toh main koi safai nahi dunga isme. Bas 3 cheezein kehna chahunga:

  • You are the only person in the world who has seen my most vulnerable side (not even Raj!), only because you hold a very special place in my life ❤️.
  • I am really sorry 🥺.
  • Ki jab tak dosti hai, tab tak toh in cheezon se thoda bahut pareshaan karta hi rahunga! 👻😂
Chapter 2

The End Credits (Thank You)

Kisi mahaan kavi ne kaha hai (abhi main us kavi ka naam bhool gaya hoon, but kya kaha hai is important)—ki ek ladki ko dost banake apne paas humesha rakhna chahiye, kyunki wo kuch sikhaye na sikhaye, shaoor zaroor sikha deti hai 😌. Aur meri zindagi mein wo 'vyakti vishesh' aap hain, jisne mereko kaise bolna chahiye, kya kab bolna chahiye, aur bhi kai bhinn-bhinn prakar ki cheezein sikhayi hain. Aapko pata nahi hoga ki aapne sikhaya hai, but sikhaya hai! 🧐

Jaise ki:

  • Jab bhi aap poore mood se kuch serious bata rahi hon, aur main beech mein bol doon, toh maine gaali khayi hai 🤐. Toh isse ye samajh aa gaya ki beech mein nahi bolna chahiye!
  • Jab bhi kisi case mein samne wali mahila mitr ne apko dost ko clean chit de di ho, toh kripa karke apne dost ko bhi clean chit dilane ki koshish na karein 🛑🙅‍♂️.
  • Mahila mitr is always right! Agar usne bola hai ki usne haathi udta dekha hai, toh aapko kehna chahiye, 'Haan, maine toh uski sawaari bhi ki hai!' 🐘✈️😂

Chalo, jokes aside. I know I rarely say thank you to you, but I want to say thank you very much, Sejal. Thank you for controlling my overthinking problem 🧠, for being there at times when most people just disappear 🫂, and for being the person who genuinely gets happy about my achievements 🌟.

Before we started talking, I had faced multiple betrayals from friends. I literally thought after that ki I will not make anyone a friend anymore; everyone will just be a classmate, colleague, or an acquaintance. But when I saw your behavior towards me and towards your other friends, I got my trust in friendship back ❤️🩹.

And yes, I forgot the most important point—thank you for keeping my vulnerable side safe with you 🔐. Thank you for bearing with me, my jokes, and sometimes my totally out-of-context text messages 📱😅.

Chapter 3

Season 2 Teaser (My Requests)

Aage aane wale saalon ke liye, I just want ki humara bond itna strong ho jaye ki tum chah kar bhi mujhe ignore na kar pao 😉. Humari calls, VCs, aur mulakatein itni frequent honi chahiye ki you actually start missing me the moment we hang up. Yeh jo hum saal-saal bhar mein mil rahe hain na, ab isse kaam nahi chalega—you definitely need to see this face more often! 😎 Ab se Delhi aao toh tumhare schedule mein meri VIP booking honi chahiye. Aur main toh Pune aata hi rahunga tumhe tang karne ke excuses dhoondh ke... at least until you finally give in and take that transfer to Delhi permanently! 😏🤞✨

And yes, there is a small request on board 😊😌: Sejal, I know sometimes my behavior towards you becomes very weird, which at some point might have hurt you—bhale hi wo pehle kai baar hua ho ya recently. Aur sach bataun toh, main yeh sab ajeeb harkatein sirf tumhare saamne karta hoon. Kyunki tum mere liye koi normal friend nahi ho... you are so much more than that to me. It just happens when my overthinking takes over my mind, and I get this fear ki I will lose the person or that they will drift away. Isiliye my texts get weird, my mind stops working, and I just start texting anyone on my list ki koi mere se baat karta rahe. And I just want to request you: agar aisa kabhi ho, toh please... tumhara mereko daantne ka mann kare daant lo, gaali deni ho gaali de lo, in fact, maarna-peetna ho toh you can do that too! But yaar, bas uss samay thodi der meri bak-bak sun liya karo 🥺🙏.

And I know ki yeh sentence thoda ajeeb sound karega, and I also know ki aisa kabhi nahi hoga, but please... kabhi trust mat todna. Kyunki sach bolun toh, while overthinking, I have thought of every possible situation, but the one thing I cannot even imagine is how I will survive if you and that mota guy break my trust. Because like I always say, you two are the last people I trust blindly 🔐❤️.

Now, I am going to be dead serious for a second. I only have one real complaint about you, and it is something I need you to stop doing immediately. You know perfectly well that I can read your mood just by the way you text. I always know when something is wrong. But you still try to put up a wall and hide it. You have gone through some incredibly heavy, serious situations and chose to carry that weight alone instead of telling me.

That stops now.

My ultimate demand for 'Season 2' of this friendship is this: Stop shutting me out when things get hard. You do not have to protect me from your bad days. I am not a part-time friend who is only here for the jokes and the good vibes. I am here for the messy, difficult, and painful stuff, too. Let me be there for you the exact same way you are always there for me. No more hiding. Deal?

Tumhe bas ek call ya text karna hai, and I will be there. Bhale hi tum Pune mein ho, Delhi mein ho, ya duniya ke kisi bhi kone mein... I will drop everything and reach you ASAP.
